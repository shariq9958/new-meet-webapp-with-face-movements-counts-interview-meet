Answers to Your Questions

How does the AI analysis improve hiring decisions?

Our AI provides objective data points on candidate engagement, communication clarity, and other soft skills that are hard to quantify from a resume alone. This helps reduce bias and identify top performers more consistently.

Can we integrate YourMeetApp with our existing ATS?

ATS integration is on our roadmap for Professional and Enterprise plans. We aim to provide seamless connections with popular Applicant Tracking Systems to streamline your entire recruitment workflow. Contact us for specific inquiries.

What kind of support do you offer for company plans?

All company plans include dedicated support. Professional plans receive priority email and chat support, while Enterprise plans benefit from a dedicated account manager and custom SLAs for premium assistance.
